Our Mission

Our main goal through flowtivity is to promote efficient and productive habits to our users and those who generally struggle to be productive or are easily distracted.

For example, some students never learned how to study properly, therefore higher education may be more of a challenge, and we're here to help them with that challenge.

We also strive to help those with conditions that may affect their ability to focus, so through our website, we hope to help others achieve their goals more proactively.
